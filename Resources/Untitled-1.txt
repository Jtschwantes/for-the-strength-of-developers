Prepare
1. Commit Team Members: Course Designer, Students that work for the Course Designer, Course Lead (campus faculty)
Develop
- Design and Build the Course

Review
- Part 1: Christy Owens team reviews the Course for errors, legal, and ADA compliance
- Part 2: Designers make any fixes necessary
- Part 3: Decision is made if the Course is ready to pilot

Pilot
- Typically 1 section of the Course is ran with actual Students
- The Course Designer works with the Course Lead and a student from Course Support and the Online Instructor to 
make any changes while the course is running
